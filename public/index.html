UNO No Mercy

UNO No Mercy is a ruthless version of the classic card game! With relentless Wild Draw 6, Draw 10, and Roulette cards, the action never stops. The rules are brutal, stacking is allowed, and if you get 25 cards in your hand, you're out!

How to Play:

  • Create a Room: Enter your name and click 'Create Room'. Share the generated Room ID with your friends.
  • Join a Room: Enter your name, the Room ID provided by your friend, and click 'Join Room'.
  • Start Game: The host (creator) can click 'Start Game' once everyone has joined.

Quick Rules:

  • 7s Swap: Playing a 7 forces you to swap your hand with another player.
  • 0s Pass: Playing a 0 forces ALL players to pass their hands to the next player.
  • Stacking: Draw cards can be stacked to punish the next player!
  • Elimination: Cross 25 cards in hand? You're immediately eliminated!

For complete rules, check the Rule Book in the lobby.

    Special Rules
    7's Swap - When you play a 7 card of any color, you MUST swap your hand with another player of your choice. Play then continues in current order.
    0's Pass - When you play a 0 card of any color, ALL players must pass their hand to the next player in the current direction of play.
    Wild Cards
    Wild Draw 4 - Reverse the direction of play and force the next player to draw 4 cards. You can choose the next color.
    Wild Draw 6 - Force the next player to draw 6 cards. You can choose the next color.
    Wild Draw 10 - Force the next player to draw 10 cards. You can choose the next color.
    Roulette - The next player must draw cards until they draw a card of the color you choose. You can choose the color.
    Action Cards
    Draw 2 - Force the next player to draw 2 cards. Stacking is allowed.
    Draw 4 - Force the next player to draw 4 cards. Stacking is allowed.
    Skip - Skip the next player's turn.
    Skip All - Skip all other players' turns, effectively skipping back to your turn.
    Reverse - Reverse the direction of play.
    Discard All - Discard all cards of the current color from your hand.
    Elimination Rule
    If a player's hand reaches 25 or more cards, they are eliminated from the game.
